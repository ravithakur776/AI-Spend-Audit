# Metrics Framework

## North Star Metric
**Total Dollars Saved for Users (Cumulative Monthly Savings Identified)**

Why:
- Directly reflects customer value
- Easy to communicate in sales and marketing
- Tightly tied to retention and expansion

## Secondary Metric 1
**Audit Completion Rate**
- Formula: Completed Audits / Started Audits
- Target (MVP): >= 45%
- Why: Measures funnel quality and onboarding friction

## Secondary Metric 2
**Lead-to-Paid Conversion Rate**
- Formula: Paid Accounts / Captured Leads
- Target (MVP): >= 10%
- Why: Validates pricing, ICP fit, and sales execution

## Weekly Dashboard (Minimum)
- New leads captured
- Completed audits
- Total monthly savings identified
- Paid conversions
