# GTM Strategy (Zero Budget): First 100 B2B Users

## Positioning
AI Spend Audit helps engineering teams cut wasted AI subscription spend in under 5 minutes, then gives an optimized plan with measurable savings.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Team size: 5 to 80 engineers
- Stage: Seed to Series B
- Roles: CTO, VP Eng, Eng Manager, Head of Platform
- Pain: AI tooling sprawl, overlapping plans, unclear ROI

## Distribution Plan (0-$100 users)

### 1) Founder-Led Sales (Days 1-30)
- Build a list of 150 target companies from LinkedIn + YC Directory + Product Hunt.
- Send 20 personalized outbound messages/day from founder account.
- Offer "free 15-minute AI spend teardown" for each team.
- Goal: 25 live calls, 40 audits completed.

### 2) LinkedIn Content + DM Engine
- Post 4x/week:
  - "How one 12-person team wasted $1,140/mo on AI tools"
  - "3 fastest levers to reduce Copilot/Cursor/Claude overlap"
  - "AI spend benchmark by team size"
- Every post CTA: "Comment AUDIT and I’ll send the calculator."
- DM responders with short onboarding link.
- Goal: 30-40 qualified audits.

### 3) Developer Communities (Reddit + Discord)
- Share useful teardown content, not promo-first links.
- Target communities:
  - r/ExperiencedDevs
  - r/devops
  - r/startups
  - founder/devtool Discord servers
- Publish "AI Spend Benchmark Template" and include audit link.
- Goal: 20-30 audits from community channels.

### 4) Warm Intros + Customer Proof Loop
- Ask every satisfied user for 2 intros to other Eng leaders.
- Convert best audit outcomes into anonymized case studies.
- Repost proof snippets in DMs + LinkedIn.
- Goal: 10-20 audits from referrals.

## Weekly Operating Cadence
- 100 outbound messages/week
- 4 LinkedIn posts/week
- 3 community contributions/week
- 10 follow-up calls/week

## Milestones to 100 Users
- Week 1: 10 completed audits
- Week 2: 25 completed audits
- Week 3: 50 completed audits
- Week 4-5: 100 completed audits
