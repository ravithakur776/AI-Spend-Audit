# Unit Economics

## Pricing Model Options

## Plan A: Subscription
- Free one-time audit
- Pro Monitoring: $99/month
- Includes monthly re-audit, drift alerts, optimization recommendations

## Plan B: Savings Share
- Free audit
- Implementation plan + tracking
- 10% of verified monthly savings (min $99/month)

## Recommended MVP Pricing
Start with Plan A ($99/month) for simplicity and predictable revenue.
Add savings-share as enterprise/custom option after traction.

## Assumptions (MVP)
- Average customer lifespan: 14 months
- Gross margin: 85%
- Conversion from completed audit to paid: 12%
- Average ACV (annual): $1,188 ($99 x 12)

## LTV (Theoretical)
- Monthly gross profit per customer: $99 x 0.85 = $84.15
- LTV = $84.15 x 14 months = $1,178.10

## CAC (Theoretical, Founder-Led)
- Tools + infra cost for first 100 users: ~$300
- Founder time not fully costed in cash CAC at MVP stage
- Paid conversions from 100 users: 12 customers
- Cash CAC = $300 / 12 = $25 per paying customer

## LTV:CAC
- LTV:CAC = $1,178 / $25 = 47.1x

## Reality Check
Even with a more conservative CAC of $150, LTV:CAC is ~7.9x, still strong for early B2B SaaS.
